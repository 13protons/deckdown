Deckdown

Deckdown is a totally new way to build a slide deck. You write markdown, and deckdown gives you a presentation.

No BS. Or JS. Or HTML.

Try It:

Note: This must be an actual .md file. For github, this means copying the url after clicking the 'raw' button near the top of a file's page.

Usage

Tell deckdown the url of your raw .md file by passing it as the src parameter, like this: 

http://deckdown.herokuapp.com/deck?src=path/to/yourdeck.md

For example, here's a slideshow version of the deckdown readme.

Caveats

Deckdown is still in an early experimental state. Feel free to use it for your presentations if you wish, just know that the service will likely change in the future.
